"And they (women) have rights similar to those (of men) over them in kindness." — Qur'an 2:228

What is this guide?

✦

The Nikah contract is one of the most important legal agreements a Muslim woman will ever enter. Yet many women are never told what rights it gives them — rights granted directly by Islamic law.

This guide explains those rights clearly and honestly, drawing on established scholarship. Choose your language below to read the full guide.
